    Welcome to Black Jack!
In this game you will play agianst a computer. The goal of the game is to have your hand total as close to 21 but not over.
All numerical cards count at face value and face cards (king, queen, jack) count as 10. 
Aces are 1 or 11, you will be propted to chose 1 or 11 for your ace should you have one.
You will start with 10 points and can wager them on the game and the computer will match.
If you win you recive your wagered points plus the computer's wagered points.
You will also have the choice to receive another card (a "hit"). 
Then the cards will be compared and whoever wins receives the pot. You may carry your points
into another game should you decice to play again.
Have Fun!
